What we do

We craft web and mobile applications for startups and large companies wanting to operate as entrepreneurs. We work proactively with customers to help reach their goals right from Project Inception, Design, Software Development through to post-release technical support. Our focus on creating value means that we help clients launch quickly with a codebase that can grow with them and their business.

The Builders

The name Abakhi is a Xhosa word which means "Builders". Both Kirk and I had done startups before, and we both had varying levels of success and failures. For Kirk, he was formerly CTO of Zottech and Osiki, the mobile-based polling app. Mawuli started his first company at 16 back in high school designing websites for people and campus events. We are both hustlers and believe that having good software shouldn't mean waiting for years of development, unnecessary meetings and major deployments. We love Erlang and Elixir, very very much, but our tech stack is quite broad -- NodeJs, AngularJS, Python (Django, Flask etc), Android, Go, iOS (Swift and React Native) and whatever we need to get the job done.
